What is OpenMaths in a Nutshell?

Simply put, it is a LaTeX editor, viewer, chalkboard, wiki, grammar checker, proof checker, search engine, cross-referencing tool, version tracker, history tracker, game, collaboration tool, and more, all wrapped up into a single beautiful, elegant, easy-to-use free online tool that requires absolutely no IT, programming or extra-mathematical skills.
